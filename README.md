## ✦ About

This is a **PromptWare** submission showcasing the art of prompt engineering through AI-generated visual art. The artwork celebrates the rich cultural heritage of **Odisha, India** — weaving together six distinct cultural elements into a single, breathtaking composition.

## 🖼️ Cultural Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 💃 **Odissi Dance** | Classical dance form from Odisha's temples, featuring the iconic *tribhanga* (three-bend) pose |
| ☸️ **Konark Sun Temple** | 13th-century UNESCO World Heritage Site — the stone chariot wheel is an engineering marvel |
| 🛕 **Jagannath Rath Yatra** | One of the world's oldest and grandest religious chariot festivals |
| 🎨 **Dashavatara Pattachitra** | Traditional cloth scroll painting depicting the ten avatars of Lord Vishnu |
| 🦩 **Chilika Lake** | Asia's largest brackish water lagoon — home to over a million migratory birds |
| ✍️ **Saura Tribal Art** | Ancient geometric wall paintings by the Saura tribe of southern Odisha |

## 🧠 The Prompt

```
Create a hyper-detailed, cinematic artwork celebrating the cultural heritage of Odisha, 
India. The composition should feature an Odissi dancer in a traditional tribhanga pose at 
the center, wearing authentic Sambalpuri silk with silver filigree jewelry. Behind her, 
place the iconic stone chariot wheel of the Konark Sun Temple with intricate carvings. On 
the left, depict the grand Jagannath Rath Yatra procession with colorful chariots and 
devotees. Include a Dashavatara pattachitra scroll unfurling in the foreground. In the 
background, show Chilika Lake at golden hour with flamingos in flight and traditional boats. 
The bottom border features tribal Saura art motifs. The sky is a dramatic sunset with warm 
golden and amber tones. Style: photorealistic digital art with the richness of classical 
Indian miniature painting.
```

### Prompt Engineering Techniques

- **🎯 Spatial Composition** — Precise placement directives (center, left, background, foreground) to control visual hierarchy
- **🎨 Style Anchoring** — Combining "photorealistic digital art" with "classical Indian miniature painting" for a hybrid aesthetic
- **📐 Cultural Specificity** — Named authentic elements (tribhanga, Sambalpuri silk, Saura art, pattachitra) for accuracy
- **🌅 Atmospheric Direction** — Lighting and mood cues ("golden hour", "dramatic sunset", "warm golden and amber tones")
